Accessibility

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Cozytek aims to make its website and products more accessible and usable for a broad range of people and situations.

Current Approach

This website is designed with semantic HTML, responsive layouts, readable text sizing, and straightforward navigation. Cozytek aims to follow practical accessibility best practices as the site evolves.

Conformance Status

This website has not yet undergone a formal accessibility audit. Cozytek is working toward broadly accessible patterns informed by WCAG guidance, but full conformance has not been independently assessed.

Known Limitations

As a lightweight static site, accessibility improvements may be made incrementally over time. If a future feature or page creates an accessibility barrier, Cozytek intends to address it as part of ongoing site updates.

Feedback

A dedicated accessibility contact method is not currently published on this website. Where a product-specific support option is available, that is the best current path for reporting accessibility issues related to that product.
