Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Cozytek builds software for learning, building, and creativity. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use Cozytek websites and apps, how that information may be used, and how to contact us.

Information We May Collect

Depending on the product and feature set, Cozytek may collect limited information such as:

  • account or contact information you choose to provide
  • app usage and feature interaction data
  • learning progress, performance, or completion data
  • device, diagnostic, and crash information
  • preferences and settings stored for product functionality

How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • provide and improve Cozytek apps, services, and features
  • save your progress, preferences, and product state
  • understand product performance and reliability
  • respond to support requests and product inquiries
  • maintain security and prevent misuse

Analytics

Cozytek does not currently use third-party analytics on this website. Future Cozytek apps or services may use limited analytics or diagnostic tooling to improve performance, reliability, and product quality. If those practices materially change, this Privacy Policy may be updated.

Data Storage

Some information may be stored locally on your device or in systems used to support app functionality, account features, sync, or progress tracking.

Data Sharing

Cozytek does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers or infrastructure partners as reasonably necessary to operate, secure, and improve Cozytek products and services.

Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain information, though some features may not function properly without it. You may also contact Cozytek regarding questions about your data.

Children’s Privacy

Cozytek products are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

Cozytek may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
